- Massive amounts of breathing room
- Whitespace as primary element
- Content has space to exist

### 3. **Precision & Alignment**
- Everything aligned to strict grid
- Careful spacing ratios
- Nothing arbitrary

### 4. **Refined Typography**
- Small, light weight fonts
- Wide letter-spacing (tracking-[0.3em])
- Lowercase with selective uppercase
- Font-light, not font-bold

### 5. **Product > Designer**
- Work showcased, not personality
- Humble presentation
- Let achievements speak
